NEDA Helpline

The National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) Helpline provides support, resources, and guidance for those struggling with eating disorders and loved ones.

Self-Compassion (Dr. Kristin Neff)

Dr. Kristin Neff's Self-Compassion site offers tools, credible research, and guided practices to encourage individuals to build self-kindness, resilience, and emotional well-being.

The Body Positive

The Body Positive promotes self-acceptance and body empowerment through education, workshops, and resources focused on healing body image issues.

Project HEAL

Project HEAL provides financial assistance, advocacy, and support programs to help people access treatment for eating disorders (especially those facing barriers posed by the system).

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

The 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline offers free, confidential 24/7 support for anyone experiencing distress, suicidal thoughts, or a mental health crisis.
